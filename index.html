About Me

Hi! My name is Colter Giem, and I'm a flat designer and web developer from Denver, Colorado. I love making beautiful, functional websites with a balance between aesthetic and simplicity, and I hope to satisfy any client needs. I work primarily with Javascript, including JQuery and AngularJS, as well as graphics made in Inkscape. If you want to check out my Github or Dribbble, go right ahead.
